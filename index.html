National Mission For Clean Ganga

With the installation of two new Sewer Treatment Plants under National Mission for Clean Ganga, the sewage treatment capacity of Haridwar city has been increased to 145 million litre per day which is adequate for treating the 108 million litres of sewage being generated in the city per day.



What Does The World Health Organization Do?

The World Health Organization describes its job as "the global guardian of health." It is now possibly facing the most devastating global health threat in its 72-year history: the coronavirus pandemic. WHO is devoting hundreds of millions of dollars and an all-hands-on-deck approach to the effort to vanquish the virus. On April 14, President Trump put a temporary halt on U.S. funding for the agency, pending an investigation to see if WHO is "severely mismanaging and covering up the spread of the coronavirus."



KKR vs DC Highlights, IPL 2019: Shikhar Dhawan stars as Delhi Capitals beat Kolkata Knight Riders by 7 wickets

DC, batting first, put up 161/7 in 20 overs. They were given some early jolts by Jofra Archer (3/19), as they were reduced to 10/2 in the third over. Shikhar Dhawan (57) counter-attacked against RR’s other bowlers, as DC reached the end of the Powerplay with 47/2. Shreyas Iyer (53) took charge after Dhawan’s wicket. RR were given a cracking start by Jos Buttler (22) and Ben Stokes (41), and Sanju Samson (25) built a steadying partnership with Stokes in the middle overs. Anrich Nortje (2/33) delivered the killer blow in the death overs with Robin Uthappa’s (32) wicket, after which RR could not keep up with the required run rate. They eventually reached 148/8 in their 20 overs, losing by 13 runs.
